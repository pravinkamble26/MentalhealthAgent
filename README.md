# 🧠 Mental Health Awareness & Suicide Prevention Agent

## Project Overview

Mental Health Awareness & Suicide Prevention Agent is an AI-powered support platform developed using **IBM Cloud** and **IBM watsonx Orchestrate** to promote mental well-being, increase awareness, and provide early emotional support.

The platform is designed to help individuals facing challenges such as **stress, anxiety, emotional distress, depression awareness, and suicidal thoughts** by offering supportive conversations, wellness guidance, and preventive mental health assistance.

---

## Problem Statement

Mental health issues such as **depression, anxiety, emotional distress, and suicidal thoughts** are increasing globally. Social stigma, lack of awareness, and limited access to timely support prevent many individuals from seeking help.

Traditional support systems are often reactive rather than preventive, creating gaps in:

* Early emotional distress detection
* Mental health awareness
* Immediate access to guidance
* Continuous emotional well-being support

There is a need for an intelligent and accessible platform that encourages users to seek support earlier and promotes healthier coping mechanisms.

---

## Proposed Solution

This project introduces an **AI-powered Mental Health Awareness and Suicide Prevention Agent** that delivers supportive, personalized, and preventive mental wellness assistance.

Built on **IBM Cloud** and **IBM watsonx Orchestrate**, the system coordinates intelligent workflows and enables smooth interactions between conversational AI components.

The platform:

* Provides mental health awareness information
* Supports emotional wellness conversations
* Suggests healthy coping strategies
* Encourages users to seek professional help when appropriate
* Promotes preventive care and early support
